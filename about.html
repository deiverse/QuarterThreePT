About Me

Hi, I’m Majadeah E. Abdullah, a passionate photographer with a deep love for capturing the stories that surround us. With the help of photography, I explore the beauty in the ordinary and the magic in fleeting moments.

Photography isn’t just about taking pictures for me; it’s about immortalizing emotions, perspectives, and experiences. I aim to create images that speak to the heart and soul. Being able to capture every moment brings me peace and happiness. Even small events that happen are very important for me to capture, as it’s a way of preserving different experiences. I transform moments into timeless memories. From a candid smile to a spectacular view, every photograph is a reflection of life’s raw beauty.
